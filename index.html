About Me

Determined, creative, and a natural problem solver.

I'm a recent graduate from General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive program. Prior to this I've spent most of my adult life working as a hair stylist. I know that finding the connection between the two may be hard to see at first, but the similarities may surprise you. I consider myself to specialize in hair coloring, which is much more of a science than an art. It requires a thorough knowledge of the product and how it interacts with the medium. Additionally, you must also be able to communicate with the client to determine exactly what it is they are looking for. You must take all of this information and figure out how you can deliver the desired result. It's seldom a straight forward solution. I feel like I've been able to apply all the experience with communication and problem solving and apply it to what I'm truly passionate about: coding and technology.
